# The Grand Tapeo

You are out for tapas with the crew and everyone orders **at the same time**: each player
secretly picks one tapa from their tray, all picks are flipped at once, and the trays
**rotate to the left**. The game lasts **3 rounds**; the most points wins.

## How ordering works

1. Pick a card from your hand with **Enter** — your pick is secret and you can **change
   it** while anyone is still deciding.
2. Once the whole table has ordered, every pick is revealed at once onto each player's table.
3. Your tray (whatever you didn't pick) passes to the player on your left, and you receive
   the one from your right. When the trays run out, the round is scored.

## The tapas

- **Skewers** (olive skewer 1, omelette 2, prawn 3): worth their points as is.
- **Brava sauce**: waits on your table and **triples** the next skewer you help yourself
  to. Worth nothing alone.
- **Croquettes**: every **pair** is worth 5 points (a lone one, nothing).
- **Montaditos**: every **trio** is worth 10 points (loose ones, nothing).
- **Bowl of olives**: the more bowls the better — 1/3/6/10/15 points for 1/2/3/4/5+.
- **Tapas trays** (1, 2 or 3 servings): at round end, whoever gathered the most servings
  gains 6; the runner-up, 3. A tie up top splits the 6 and nobody takes the 3.
- **Homemade flan**: kept until the **end of the game** — most flans gains 6, fewest
  loses 6 (in a two-player game nobody loses points).
- **Serving tongs**: they wait on your table. Spend them to take **two tapas in one
  turn** (switch to multi-select with **Ctrl+Space**, mark two cards with Space and send
  with Enter; the first marked card is served first — a sauce marked before a skewer
  multiplies it in that very play). The tongs then rejoin the tray you pass: your
  neighbour inherits them. Worth no points on their own.

## Fine print

- Nobody sees your tray or your pick: the table only hears **who** has already ordered.
- The brava sauce only catches skewers served on **later turns**.
- Final tiebreaker: most flans wins.

Press **S** for how you are doing (points, your table and your flans); **Shift+S** sweeps
the rival tables in one go. Press **Ctrl+F1** for the full keyboard shortcut list.
