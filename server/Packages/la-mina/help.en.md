# The Mine

Be the last miner standing. On your turn you may **play any action cards you like** and then
you **must dig one card** from the deck to end your turn. Draw **firedamp** you cannot **cut the
fuse** on and you are buried and out. The last miner left in the mine wins.

## The cards

- **Firedamp**: dig it without cutting the fuse and you blow up, out of the mine.
- **Cut the fuse**: cancels the firedamp you just dug and tucks it back into the gallery at a
  depth you choose. You start with one in hand.
- **Leave the shaft**: end your turn without digging.
- **Cave-in**: end without digging and make the next miner dig extra.
- **The canary**: privately look at the coming seams on top of the deck.
- **Gallery reshuffle**: shuffle the deck; no one remembers the order anymore.
- **Borrowed pick**: a miner you choose hands you one of their cards.
- **No way!**: the only out-of-turn card; cancels a pending play. A "no way" can itself be
  "no-wayed".
- **Mine critters** (rat, bat, mole, worm, beetle): no power alone; **two of the same** discard
  as a pair to steal a random card from a miner you choose.

## The match

Play until a single un-buried miner remains. There is one pocket of firedamp fewer in the deck
than there are miners: sooner or later someone digs one.

When someone plays an action there is a beat of suspense — a rising tone — in which anyone
holding a "no way!" can cut it. React with the **reaction key** before the tone reaches the top.

Nobody shouts here: every player's card count is **yours to check whenever you want** — **S**
tells your own story and **Shift+S** sweeps the rivals in one go. Press **Ctrl+F1** for the full
shortcut list.
