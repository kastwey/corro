# The Wheel of Wits

Answer questions from six categories, earn a wedge at each colour headquarters, and return to the centre for the final question.

## Help during play

- **F1** opens this Wheel of Wits guide.
- **Ctrl+F1** opens the complete list of shortcuts available in this game.
- **Ctrl+Shift+F1** shows the active rules, including judge type, difficulty and winning conditions.

## Your turn
- **Space**: roll the die.
- After rolling, a list of the possible destinations opens (you can **drag** it by its title or **minimize** it to see the whole board): confirm one there, or press **Escape** to go to the board — the reachable squares are highlighted — cycle them with **D** (**Shift+D** to go back) and press **Enter** on the one you want. With a mouse, just **click the highlighted square**.
- On landing you get a question of that category. **Type your answer**: it is read out to the table and the judge decides whether it is correct.
- Correct: if you are on a colour headquarters you earn that wedge, and you **roll again**. Wrong: the turn passes.
- Collect all six wedges, return to the centre, and answer the final question to win.

## Exploring the board (does not move your piece)
- **Arrows**: up moves toward the centre, down toward the ring, left and right turn.
- **E**: go to the centre.
- **L**: where you are on the circle — the clock hour when you are on a spoke or a junction (the spokes sit at 12, 2, 4, 6, 8 and 10 o'clock), or the region of the ring (top, lower right…) between the hours.
- **Cycle a colour** (English colour initial): **B** Geography, **P** Entertainment, **Y** History, **R** Arts & Literature, **G** Science, **O** Sports. The first press goes to the headquarters; pressing again walks the other squares of that colour (**Shift+letter** to go back).
- **S**: your wedges. **Shift+S**: everyone else's.
- **M**: your piece. **N** / **Shift+N**: cycle the pieces on the table.
- **C**: announce your piece and colour.

Press **Ctrl+F1** for the full list of keys.

## Playing with a screen reader

### Exploring and choosing a destination

- **Escape** returns focus to the wheel. The arrows, **E**, **L**, colour letters, **M** and **N** move the exploration cursor and announce each node without moving your piece.
- **Space** rolls the die. When the destination choice opens, use its buttons or press **Escape**, cycle the highlighted destinations with **D** and **Shift+D**, and confirm with **Enter**. **Ctrl+D** returns to the pending dialog.
- While typing an answer, the text box keeps letter keys so no game shortcut interferes.

### Checking the game

- **S** announces your wedges, **Shift+S** everyone else's, **C** your piece and colour, and **T** says whose turn it is. **Ctrl+P** moves to the players list.
- Questions, answers, judge decisions, wedges and turn changes are announced automatically.

### Moving between areas and using chat

- **F6** cycles through the wheel, actions, players and connection; non-modal dialogs and chat join while open. **Shift+F6** moves through the areas in reverse.
- **Ctrl+Shift+R** opens chat and focuses the message box. **Ctrl+Shift+H** opens or closes chat.
- This guide, the shortcuts and the active rules are reading documents. Use your screen reader's heading and list commands, then close them with **Escape** to return to play.
