# Galactic Empire

Welcome to the **Galactic Empire**, a space-themed take on the classic
property-trading game. Conquer star systems, charge tolls to anyone who lands on
them, and drive your rivals into bankruptcy. The last solvent player wins.

## Goal

Be the only player who hasn't gone bankrupt. Buy planets, build colonies, and
collect from anyone who lands on your territory.

## Currency

This board is played in **credits (₡)**, not euros.

## Your turn

1. **Roll the dice** (Space). Your ship advances by the total rolled.
2. Depending on where you land:
   - **Unowned planet**: you may buy it. If you decline, it goes to auction.
   - **Another player's planet**: you pay the toll (rent) to its owner.
   - **Card square**: draw from *Fortune* or the *Black Market* and apply it.
   - **Tax**: pay the listed amount to the bank.
3. Rolling **doubles** lets you roll again. Three doubles in a row send you to the Black Hole.
4. When you're done, **end your turn** (Enter).

Each time you complete a lap and pass the **Launch Pad** you collect your salary.

## Special squares

- **Launch Pad**: the start square. You collect when you pass it.
- **Black Hole**: where you're held. Just visiting is harmless; held, you pay the release cost,
  use a release card, or wait to roll doubles to get out.
- **Tractor Beam**: drags you straight to the Black Hole.
- **Asteroid Belt**: a free resting stop — nothing happens.

## Groups and connections

- Planets are grouped by **system** (colour). Control a whole system to build
  **colonies**, which raise the toll sharply.
- **Hyperspace Jumps** (the "stations") pay more the more you own.
- **Supplies** (the "utilities") charge based on your dice roll.

## Building

Once you own every planet in a system, invest to build and multiply the toll.
Sell buildings or mortgage planets if you need credits to settle a debt.

## Winning

The game ends when only one solvent player remains. May the galaxy be yours!
