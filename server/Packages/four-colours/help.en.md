# Four Colours

Shed every card before anyone else. On your turn, play **one card that fits** the top of
the discards — by **colour**, by **number** or by **action** (a skip on a skip, even
across colours) — or **draw one** (Space). If the drawn card fits, you may play it right
away (Enter) or keep it and pass (Space again).

## Help during play

- **F1** opens this Four Colours guide.
- **Ctrl+F1** opens the complete shortcut list for this game's active rules.
- **Ctrl+Shift+F1** shows the active rules, such as draw stacking, doubles and the last-card call.
- With a card in your hand focused, **Shift+F1** opens its help and explains what it does and when it can be played.

## The cards

- **Numbers (0–9)**: score their value at the count.
- **Skip**: the next player loses their turn.
- **Reverse**: the direction flips (with two players it acts as a skip).
- **Draw two**: the next player draws 2 and loses their turn.
- **Wild**: always playable; you name the colour in force.
- **Wild draw four**: like the wild, and the next player draws 4 and loses their turn.
  It may only be played while you hold **no card of the colour in force** — the table
  checks it here, so there is no bluffing and no challenge.

## The match

Emptying your hand wins the **round** and collects the points left in every rival hand
(numbers at face value, actions 20, wilds 50). Rounds repeat until someone reaches
**500**; each round's winner leads the next.

Nobody shouts here: every player's card count is **yours to check whenever you want** —
**S** tells your own story (cards, colour in force, top card and points) and **Shift+S**
sweeps the rivals in one go. Noticing who is running short is part of the game. Press
**Ctrl+F1** for the full shortcut list.

## Playing with a screen reader

### Your hand

- **Up Arrow** and **Down Arrow** move through the cards; **Home** and **End** go to the first and last. **Right Arrow** enters a card's actions, and **Escape** moves back or returns to the hand.
- **Shift+F10** opens the card menu to play it, ask for help, sort the hand or show only playable cards. **Shift+F1** opens the focused card's help directly.
- **Enter** plays the focused card. **Space** draws; after drawing a playable card, press it again to keep the card and pass.

### Finding cards and checking the table

- **D** announces the deck count, discard top and colour in force. **S** announces your hand, direction and score; **Shift+S** reviews your rivals.
- **R**, **G**, **B** and **Y** jump through cards of that colour; number keys jump through that value and **I** through special cards. Add **Shift** to move backward.
- Some rules add keys for doubles or calling the last card. **Ctrl+F1** always shows the exact list for the current game.

### Moving between areas and using chat

- **F6** cycles through the hand, players and connection; non-modal dialogs and chat join while open. **Shift+F6** moves in reverse. **Ctrl+P** goes to players.
- **Ctrl+Shift+R** opens chat and focuses the message box. **Ctrl+Shift+H** opens or closes chat.
- Plays, draws, penalties, colour changes and turns are announced automatically. Help and rules are reading documents; close them with **Escape** to return to play.
