# Starter Race

Move both pieces from home around the circuit and into your final corridor. Capture lone rivals outside safe squares and reach the goal by exact count.

## Help during play

- **F1** opens this guide.
- **Ctrl+F1** opens all current shortcuts.
- **Ctrl+Shift+F1** reads the die, barrier and bonus rules.

## Your turn

Press **Space** to roll. A forced or sole legal move happens automatically. With several legal pieces, a non-modal choice opens; **Enter** activates the focused option.

## Playing with a screen reader

**Escape** moves from a choice to the board without cancelling it; **Ctrl+D** returns to the choice. Arrows explore circuit and seat zones. **M** cycles your pieces, **N** all occupied positions, **S** route landmarks and **Home** the beginning of the current lane. Every move, capture, barrier and bonus is announced. **F6** cycles game areas and **Ctrl+Shift+R** focuses chat.
