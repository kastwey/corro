# Starter Draft

Everyone secretly picks one token at the same time, reveals together and passes the remaining hand left. After the single round, points, pairs, signals and memories are scored.

## Help during play

- **F1** opens this guide.
- **Ctrl+F1** opens the current shortcuts.
- **Ctrl+Shift+F1** reads round and scoring rules.
- **Shift+F1** explains the focused token.

## Each pick

Press **Enter** to choose the focused card. You may change the secret pick until everyone commits. There is no current player: all seats act simultaneously.

## Playing with a screen reader

Up and Down move through your private hand; Right enters actions, **Shift+F10** opens the menu and **Escape** returns to the hand. **S** announces your public table and score; **Shift+S** reviews rivals. Picks are private, while commits, reveals, hand rotation and scoring are announced. **F6** cycles game areas and **Ctrl+Shift+R** focuses chat.
