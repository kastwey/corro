# Starter Knowledge Wheel

Answer one multiple-choice question in each of six categories, collect all six wedges, then return to the centre and answer the final question.

## Help during play

- **F1** opens this guide.
- **Ctrl+F1** opens all current shortcuts.
- **Ctrl+Shift+F1** reads answer, centre and finish rules.

## Your turn

Press **Space** to roll. Choose a reachable destination in the non-modal dialog, then answer with one of the displayed choices. Correct headquarters answers earn wedges.

## Playing with a screen reader

**Escape** moves from the destination dialog to the wheel without cancelling it; **Ctrl+D** returns. Arrows explore the wheel, **E** jumps to the centre, **L** announces clock position, **D** cycles reachable destinations, **M** your piece and **N** all pieces. **S** announces your wedges and **Shift+S** rivals. Questions, choices, results and wedges are announced. **F6** cycles areas and **Ctrl+Shift+R** focuses chat.
