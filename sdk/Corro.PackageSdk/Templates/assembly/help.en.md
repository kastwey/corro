# Starter Assembly

Complete one functional amber unit and one functional blue unit. Damage disables a matching rival unit; repair restores, shields and eventually locks your own unit.

## Help during play

- **F1** opens this guide.
- **Ctrl+F1** opens all shortcuts.
- **Ctrl+Shift+F1** reads hand, discard and victory rules.
- **Shift+F1** explains the focused card.

## Your turn

Press **Enter** to play the focused card or **Delete** to discard it. The hand refills automatically. A target dialog opens when more than one legal unit or rival exists.

## Playing with a screen reader

Up and Down move through the hand; Right enters card actions, **Shift+F10** opens its menu and **Escape** returns to the hand. **S** announces both of your unit slots and **Shift+S** rival assemblies. Installations, damage, repairs, shields, locks and private refills are announced. **F6** cycles game areas and **Ctrl+Shift+R** focuses chat.
