# Starter Chain Reaction

Play any actions you want, then draw to end the turn. Drawing the unstable spark without Extinguish eliminates you. Last player standing wins.

## Help during play

- **F1** opens this guide.
- **Ctrl+F1** opens current shortcuts.
- **Ctrl+Shift+F1** reads deal, attack and reaction rules.
- **Shift+F1** explains the focused card.

## Your turn

Press **Enter** to play a focused action and **Space** to draw. Extinguishing a spark opens a secret depth picker before returning it to the pile.

## Playing with a screen reader

Up and Down move through the hand; Right enters actions, **Shift+F10** opens the menu and **Escape** returns. **C** announces pile count and discard top; **S** your state and **Shift+S** rivals. During a reaction window, **N** focuses Cancel and Enter plays it. Actions, reactions, draws and eliminations are announced. **F6** cycles areas and **Ctrl+Shift+R** focuses chat.
