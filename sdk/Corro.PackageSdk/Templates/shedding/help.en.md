# Starter Shedding Game

Empty your hand first. Match the discard by colour, number or action, or draw. A wild card lets you choose the colour in force.

## Help during play

- **F1** opens this guide.
- **Ctrl+F1** opens the exact shortcuts for active rules.
- **Ctrl+Shift+F1** reads draw and scoring rules.
- **Shift+F1** explains the focused card.

## Your turn

Press **Enter** to play the focused card. Press **Space** to draw; if that card fits, play it with Enter or press Space again to keep it and pass.

## Playing with a screen reader

Up and Down move through the hand; Right enters actions, **Shift+F10** opens the menu and **Escape** returns. **C** announces the discard top and colour. **S** announces your hand and score; **Shift+S** rival counts. Colour letters, numbers and **I** jump to matching cards; add Shift to go backward. Plays, draws, effects and turns are announced. **F6** cycles areas and **Ctrl+Shift+R** focuses chat.
